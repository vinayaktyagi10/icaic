From Draft to Print: Pre-Conference Workshop on LaTeX

Workshop Date: September 16, 2026

School of Computer Science and Engineering, Manipal University Jaipur, India is going   to   organize a one-day workshop on “From Draft  to Print: Pre-Conference Workshop   on   LaTeX” under   the   aegis   of   First   International   Conference   on Artificial   Intelligence   &   Computing (ICAIC-2026). The   aim of the workshop is to help participants to structure the scientific content in a proper manner and also discuss new tools of scientific publishing. Participants will be benefited from our eminent experts who have contributed greatly to the research society by publishing plenty of Research Articles/ Books etc.

 

Who should attend: Students, Research Scholars, Faculty Members, Industry participants, etc

 

Purpose/ Benefits:

  • To learn the basics of research paper writing (How to prepare a manuscript, How to select a Journal for your work, Submission ethics, Reference management, etc.)
  • To enhance the paper writing skills.
  • To learn about various tools (LaTeX, Mendeley, Zotero, etc. ) that are effective in research paper writing .
  • Do’s and Don’t of research paper writing, etc. 
  • Inaugural free access to all the distinguished keynote speaker session.
